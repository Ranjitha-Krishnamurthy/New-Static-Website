Who We Are ?

The team behind Nexurova has been the pivotal force in leveraging blockchain technology to create significant value for government organizations, particularly the Government of Telangana, India. Our acclaimed product, T-Chits, developed on Hyperledger Fabric, has been operational for the past five years and continues to expand its reach.

Through our implementations, we have been instrumental in generating value not only for governments but also for enterprises and blockchain companies. Our production-grade product facilitates seamless public, private, or hybrid deployments, making it a versatile and impactful solution for various sectors.
